Topics

Five questions clients ask before they book.

What is Russian Manicure?

A Russian manicure is a detail-led manicure technique focused on dry cuticle work, close prep, and a cleaner finished edge before color or gel is applied.

Clients usually choose it for a more precise look around the cuticle and a sharper overall finish.

Builder Gel vs Gel-X

Builder gel is best for support and structure on the natural nail. Gel-X is best for added length with a lightweight extension system.

The better choice depends on whether your priority is strength, length, or both.

How Long Do Gel-X Extensions Last?

Gel-X wear time depends on nail growth, maintenance, and daily habits, but many clients expect around two to three weeks of clean wear before a refresh or removal.

Are Russian Manicures Safe?

Russian manicures are safe when they are performed with careful technique, controlled pressure, proper tool handling, and clean sanitation practices.

Problems are more often linked to rushed work, over-filing, or poor removal habits.

Best Nail Shapes for Builder Gel

Builder gel works especially well with shapes that benefit from structure and balance, including soft almond, oval, square, and tapered square.

The best shape depends on nail length, natural growth pattern, and the finish you want.
