About Montana Nails

Designed for clients who want a more polished standard.

Montana Nails brings together clean technique, detail-driven work, and a calmer Santa Monica salon experience for clients who care about finish, service, and atmosphere.

Philosophy

The work should feel elevated before the polish even goes on.

At Montana Nails, luxury is built through thoughtful prep, refined pacing, and finishes that feel modern rather than overdone.

01

Detail obsessed

Shape, prep, surface, and shine are handled with the same level of care.

02

Modern luxury

The visual language is clean, current, and editorial instead of overly sweet or trend-heavy.

03

Hospitality feel

The goal is not just a beautiful set, but an appointment that feels polished from start to finish.

Standards

What defines the Montana Nails experience.

  • Clean service flow with attention to comfort, prep, and finish.
  • Russian manicure, builder gel, Gel-X, and elevated pedicure offerings.
  • Luxury detail and shape work for both understated and statement looks.
  • A bright boutique atmosphere on Montana Avenue in Santa Monica.
